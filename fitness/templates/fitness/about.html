About My Goals
My Goals is where you can create your own goals by clicking the "plus" circle, and have the option to "complete" it by clicking the green checkmark, or "discard" it by clicking the red "x" icon.

You can also access the Goal Progress Archive page, and the App Achievements page.
Goal Progress Archive
The Goal Progress Archive is a place you can see all the past goals you have completed by clicking on the green checkmark.

It's a place to see all your past achievements, so feel proud about yourself, your making great progress!
